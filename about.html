Who We Are

Vital-Bliss College of Health Sciences and Technology is a government-approved institution dedicated to training competent health professionals. Located in the serene environment of Lagos State, we provide a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for the modern healthcare sector.

Our mission is to illuminate minds through quality education, upholding our motto "Education is Light". We maintain high standards of academic discipline and moral integrity.
